About Us

Hear our story!


Welcome to Frying Nemo – your go-to spot for the best fish and chips on the coast!

Nestled near the beautiful shoreline, we’re a family-owned restaurant that’s passionate about serving award-winning, locally loved fish and chips. What sets us apart? We believe in doing the simple things right – starting with using only the freshest catch, hand-prepared daily, and cooked to crispy, golden perfection.

At Frying Nemo, you're not just a customer – you're part of our family. Whether you're stopping by after a day at the beach or grabbing dinner for the whole crew, we’re here to make sure every meal is a delicious, feel-good experience.

So come in, relax, and enjoy the taste of the coast – one bite at a time.
